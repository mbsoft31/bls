RESSORTISSANTS ET RÉSIDENTS TUNISIENS

Tunisian Citizens residing in Tunisia and Third Country nationals (including Libyan), who are holder of a valid Tunisian Residence Card.

RESSORTISSANTS LIBYENS

APPLICANTS HOLDING A GREEN PASSPORT (NOT BIOMETRIC ONE) WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED BY THE EMBASSY.
ALL PASSPORTS HAVE TO REMAIN AT THE EMBASSY PREMISES UNTIL THE VISA FILE IN RESOLVED (BETWEEN 10 TO 15 DAYS ).
Libyan Citizens residing in Libya.

NOTE IMPORTANTE: Appointment should be booked selecting correct Nationality, Category and by entering correct Passport details. Appointment booked under wrong category and / or with wrong details will NOT be accepted by Joint Visa Application Centre.
